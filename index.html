/// TESTIMONIAL

"Not only is Katie B. a truly talented creative, but she leaves an impression that is impossible to forget. She is compassionate, humble, hilarious, and truly one of a kind—ICONIC."

I’ve had the privilege of watching her grow into the powerful, resilient, and gracious woman she is today, spanning from her love of Bob Duncan to her passion for documentary work on aquatic apes. I first met Katie in 8th grade Spanish class through Andrew Garcia, and I’ll never forget her singing 'We finish each other's sandwiches!' to make me laugh. But a core memory for me will always be 9th grade, when she dropped the perfect Bob Duncan joke that sealed our friendship. I really got to know her when I invited her to my 15th birthday party. I was such an odd, weird kid back then—making jokes about 'Kale the Queefer'—but Katie was so open to getting to know me, just as I was to her. She has always been there to listen to me babble about my issues, offering her presence and feedback without a shred of judgment. She keeps you guessing, radiates excitement, and is simply the best. She keeps you guessing, radiates excitement, and is simply the best.

Happy Birthday, Katie

- Caleb Smith (Also known by Kale from his friends in DSM)
